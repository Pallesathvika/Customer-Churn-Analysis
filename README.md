Project Overview:
This project presents a Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard built entirely in Power BI to analyze customer behavior, identify churn patterns, and provide data-driven business insights.
The dashboard helps stakeholders understand the key drivers of churn and supports strategic decision-making to improve customer retention.

Objectives:
Analyze overall customer churn rate
Identify high-risk customer segments
Understand the impact of contract type on churn
Evaluate the relationship between support calls and churn
Examine payment delays and their effect on customer retention

Key Business Metrics:
Total Customers: 64,000
Overall Churn Rate: 47.37%
Average Customer Spend: 541.02
Average Customer Tenure: 31.99 months

Key Insights
1️⃣ Contract Type & Churn Risk
Monthly contract customers show the highest churn rate (51.61%).
Annual and Quarterly contracts demonstrate better retention.
Longer-term contracts reduce churn risk.

2️⃣ Support Calls Impact
Churn increases significantly after 5+ support calls.
High support interaction indicates potential dissatisfaction.

3️⃣ Churn by Gender
Slight variation in churn distribution between male and female customers.
Helps support demographic-based analysis.

4️⃣ Subscription Type Analysis
Basic, Standard, and Premium plans show similar churn patterns.
Suggests service quality and experience may influence churn more than plan type.
Payment behavior is a strong churn indicator.

5️⃣ Payment Delay Impact
Customers with payment delays beyond 15–20 days show significantly higher churn probability.
Payment behavior is a strong churn indicator.

Tools & Technologies Used:

Features of the Dashboard:

Business Value:
This dashboard enables organizations to:
Detect early churn warning signs
Improve contract strategy design
Optimize customer support processes
Monitor high-risk customer behavior
Develop targeted retention campaigns
